Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common resident questions. If you don’t see your question here, ask your RA or use the Resources page.

Who do I contact for maintenance issues?

Submit a maintenance request through the housing system/portal. If it’s urgent, notify staff immediately.

See “Maintenance” steps UALR Housing

What counts as an emergency maintenance issue?

Flooding, no heat/AC in extreme weather, strong electrical burning smell, serious leaks, or anything that risks safety.

Tip: If it’s a safety risk, don’t wait — report it immediately.

What are quiet hours?

Quiet hours are enforced during designated times. Even outside quiet hours, be respectful (especially late night / early morning).

Tip: Headphones + low bass = you’ll never have problems.

Can I have guests?

Guests are usually allowed within housing guidelines, but residents are responsible for guest behavior and follow all building policies.

Tip: If a guest causes issues, it still falls back on the resident.

Who do I call in an emergency?

Call 911 for immediate emergencies. You can also contact UALR Police.

UALR Police

What if I feel unsafe?

Trust your instincts. Move to a safer area, contact campus safety, and reach out to staff. If it’s immediate danger, call 911.

Tip: It’s better to be “extra safe” than to ignore warning signs.

What does my RA help with?

RAs help with community building, conflict mediation, support during difficult situations, and connecting you to campus resources. If something is outside my role, I’ll help you find the right office/person.
